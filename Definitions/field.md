$R = (X, +, \times)$ is a [[field]] when:
1. $R$ is [[commutative ring]]
2. In $X \setminus \{0\}$ there exist [[identity element]] for $\times$ (1). Where 0 is [[inverse element]] of $+$.

## Examples
- Real and Complex numbers are [[field]]
