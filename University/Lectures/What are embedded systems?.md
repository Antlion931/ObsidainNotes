## Characteristics
- Performs specific task
- Performs task in specific time frame
- High efficiency
- Minimal user interface
- Less human intervention
- They do not change rapidly
- High reliability
- Can be optimised to:
  - Have low cost
  - Be simple
  - Be easy to develop
  - Have low heating
